Lessons from KodeGo Bootcamp: Week 1

So far we have learned what soft skills are necessary to have to succeed in this bootcamp. Namely:

  • Accountability
  • Respect
  • Commitment
  • Competence
  • Consistency
  • Compassion
  • Collaboration
KodeGo emphasized not only the importance of being technically competent, but also improving one's soft skills.
